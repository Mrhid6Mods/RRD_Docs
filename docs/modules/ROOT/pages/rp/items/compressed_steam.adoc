= High Pressure Steam

High Pressure Steam is a gas produced by Modular Reactors and fed into the Super HV Turbine to produce power.

== Properties and Specifications

- **Type**: gas
- **Tier**: 7 (Refined Power Mk4)
- **Energy Value**: Not applicable
- **Byproducts**: None
- **Unlocked in**: HUB Tier 7 (Refined Power Mk4)

== Description

High Pressure Steam is generated by Modular Reactors in Steam Only or Power and Steam modes.
Production rates are detailed on the Modular Reactor page.
It is used to generate power in the Super HV Turbine.

== Usage

High Pressure Steam is fed into the Super HV Turbine to produce power.

**Tips**:
- Automate Modular Reactor output to Super HV Turbine for efficient power generation.
- Refer to Modular Reactor page for optimal production rates and modes.
