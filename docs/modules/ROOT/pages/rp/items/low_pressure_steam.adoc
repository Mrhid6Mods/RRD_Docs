= Low Pressure Steam

Low Pressure Steam is a gas byproduct produced by the Modular Reactor or Boiler, which can be dumped into the environment using a Cooling Tower or converted to Water with a Refinery.

== Properties and Specifications

- **Type**: gas
- **Energy Value**: Not applicable
- **Byproducts**: None
- **Unlocked in**: HUB Tier 5 (Advanced Modular Sizzling)

== Description

Low Pressure Steam is a byproduct generated during steam or power production in the Modular Reactor or Boiler.
Considered a waste product, it can be expelled via a link:https://docs.ficsit.app/refinedrd/latest/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Chimneys.html[Cooling Tower] or processed into Water for further use. +
