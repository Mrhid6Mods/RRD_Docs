= High Pressure Steam

High Pressure Steam is a gas produced by Boilers that can be fed into Turbine Mk.1 or Mk.2 to generate power.

== Properties and Specifications

- **Type**: gas
- **Unlocked in**: 5 (Advanced Modular Sizzling)
- **Energy Value**: Not applicable
- **Byproducts**: None

== Description

High Pressure Steam is generated by Boilers and serves as fuel for Turbine Mk.1 and Mk.2, converting thermal energy into electrical power.

== Production

Produced by Boilers.
Production rates are detailed on the Boiler UI and in the Boiler documentation.

**Tips**:
- Balance Boiler production with Turbine consumption to avoid fluid backups.
