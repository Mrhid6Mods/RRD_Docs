= Water Turbine

The Water Turbine Mk1 is a fuel-free generator that produces steady power from flowing water. Unlike most generators, it is not placed freely: it must be built on a dedicated *Water Turbine Node*, similar to how a Miner must sit on a resource node. These nodes can be found in in flowing water like rivers, creeks and waterfalls. Once placed it generates power continuously.

[NOTE]
====

"Mk1 water turbine can only be placed on dedicated nodes placed all around the world that can be found by the scanner! Generates 8, 20, 50MW depending on the node it is placed on. Some sploosh power for your factory with no item input!"
====

== Tips

* Scan for Water Turbine Nodes early. They give free, constant power with zero running cost and no logistics input.
* Prioritise *pure* nodes (50{nbsp}MW) where you can. A single pure node rivals a whole small wind farm and never drops off.
* Because output is steady, Water Turbines make excellent *baseload* to pair with the swingy output of xref:/rp/buildings/windturbines/index.adoc[Wind Turbines] and xref:/rp/buildings/solarpanels/index.adoc[Solar Panels].
* Not to be confused with the xref:/rp/buildings/waterdams/index.adoc[Water Dam], which is a separate, larger waterway-spanning structure.
