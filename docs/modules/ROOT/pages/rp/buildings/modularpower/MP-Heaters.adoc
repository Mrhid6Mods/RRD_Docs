= Modular Power Heaters

== Information:
Heaters are used to generate heat utilizing some form of fuel. The heat is used to generate steam in a xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Boilers.adoc[Boiler]. Heaters have to be placed on a *Boiler Platform*.

There are four heaters, each accepting a different family of fuel:

* *Biomass Heater:* solid biomass (wood, leaves, biofuel, alien remains…).
* *Coal Heater:* solid carbon fuels (coal, compacted coal, petroleum coke).
* *Solution Heater:* liquid/gas carbon fuels, and hot molten-salt.
* *Nuclear Heater:* fission and fusion fuel rods/cores.

Once the heater reaches 100{nbsp}℃ the attached Boiler will start producing Steam, the temperature can reach 2{nbsp}000{nbsp}℃ for the nuclear heater. 1250{nbsp}℃ for the solution heater and 500{nbsp}℃ for the biomass and coal heaters.

Burning most fuels in heaters will produce CO₂, which needs to be handled by either venting through a chimney or accumulating into Carbon Dust using a Refinery. If the CO₂ is not removed from the system and fills the heater, it will stop producing heat and slowly cool down.

[NOTE]
====
*In-game descriptions* +
*Biomass Heater:* "Used to burn any type of biofuel to be turned into heat and makes a waste product of Carbon Dioxide that needs to be vented out or used in recipes. Can produce up to 500° heat." +
*Coal Heater:* "Used to burn any type of solid carbon based fuel to be turned into heat and makes a waste product of carbon dioxide that needs to be vented out or used in recipes. Can produce up to 500° heat." +
*Solution Heater:* "Used to burn any type of liquid carbon-based fuel to be turned into heat and also makes a waste product of carbon dioxide that needs to be vented out or used in recipes. Can produce up to 750° heat." +
*Nuclear Heater:* "Can burn any type of fission or fusion-based fuel though some By-products may be made depending on the fuel. Can produce up to 2000° heat."
====

== Tips

* Keep every CO2-producing heater piped to a xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Chimneys.adoc[Co2 Chimney] (or a CO2-consuming recipe). If the CO2 output backs up, the heater stops producing heat and your boiler cools down.
* Match the heater to the boiler. A xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Boilers.adoc[Boiler Mk1] only needs 500{nbsp}°C, so a Biomass or Coal Heater is plenty. A *Boiler Mk2* is wasted below 500{nbsp}°C, pair it with a Solution or Nuclear Heater to use its higher steam production.
* Because temperature decays toward 20{nbsp}°C when unfuelled, a starved heater drags its boiler down with it. Size the fuel supply for continuous burning.
* The Solution Heater's Hot Molten Salt recipe lets a xref:/rp/buildings/solartower/index.adoc[Solar Power Tower] feed the steam system with no fuel at all.
