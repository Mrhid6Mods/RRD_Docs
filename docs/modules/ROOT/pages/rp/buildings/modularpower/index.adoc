= Modular Power

== Design Concept:
Ficsit pioneers can have a deconstructed version of any in-game power generator. We have taken each deconstructed element and created a new building for each:

* xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[Heater]
* xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Boilers.adoc[Boiler]
* xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Turbines.adoc[Turbine]
* xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Generators.adoc[Generator]
* xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Chimneys.adoc[Coolers]

== Placement:
Modular Power buildings *attach* to *platforms* that connect associated devices together, and use pipes to move resources between buildings on separate platforms.

* A xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[Heater] sits on a *Boiler Platform* with a xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Boilers.adoc[Boiler] attached. The amount of steam a *Boiler* produces is determined by the type of *Boiler* and the amount of *heat* in the *Heater*.
* A xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Turbines.adoc[Turbine] sits on a *Converter Platform* with a xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Generators.adoc[Generator] attached. The amount of power the *Generator* produces is determined by the type of *Generator* and the number of RPMs supplied by the *Turbine*
* A xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Chimneys.adoc[Cooler] sits on a *Cooling Platform* and simply vents its associated gas into the atmosphere, removing it from the system.
