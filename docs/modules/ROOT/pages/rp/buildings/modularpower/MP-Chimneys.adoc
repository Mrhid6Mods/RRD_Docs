= Modular Power Chimneys

== Information:
Modular Power Chimneys are used to vent CO₂ and Steam from xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[heaters] and xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Turbines.adoc[turbines]. They have to be placed on a Cooling Platform

== Tips

* Coolers or another means of disposing of these gasses is essential plumbing, not optional. Every heater that makes CO2 and every turbine that makes Low Pressure Steam needs somewhere for that gas to go, or the whole line stalls.
* One cooler serves several producers thanks to its high disposal rate.
* If a heater or turbine mysteriously stops, check its *output* pipe before its input: a full exhaust line is a common cause.
* Both *Low Pressure Steam* and *CO2* can be processed in a *Refinery* as an alternative to using a cooler. Low Pressure Steam can be converted to Water, and CO2 into Carbon Dust which is useful for making *Steel*.
