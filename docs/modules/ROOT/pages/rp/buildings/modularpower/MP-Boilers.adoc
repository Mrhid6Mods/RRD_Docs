= Modular Power Boilers

== Information
Boilers are placed on top of xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[Heaters] and will turn Water into the *High Pressure Steam* used by xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Turbines.adoc[Turbines]. There are currently 2 variants of boilers, Mk.1 and Mk.2.

* The Mk. 1 Boiler reaches maximum efficiency at 500 °C, so there will be heat lost when using it with Solution Heaters or Nuclear Heaters.
* To determine what the Mk2 Boiler temperature will be, consult the Fuels table for the *Heater* you are using on the xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[Heaters] page.

== Tips

* A boiler is only as hot as its heater. If steam output looks low, check the attached heater's temperature and fuel supply first.
* Don't put a Boiler Mk2 behind a Biomass/Coal Heater expecting more output — at 500{nbsp}°C it runs exactly like a Mk1.
* A xref:/rp/buildings/solartower/index.adoc[Solar Power Tower] in water mode is a drop-in replacement for a boiler: it produces the same High Pressure Steam directly from sunlight, no heater or fuel required.
