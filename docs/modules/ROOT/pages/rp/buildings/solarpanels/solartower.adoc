= Solar Power Tower

The Solar Power Tower is a concentrated-solar building that works like a *solar-powered boiler*. On its own it does nothing: it must be surrounded by xref:/rp/buildings/solarpanels/solar-power-mirror.adoc[Solar Power Mirrors], which reflect sunlight onto the tower and heat it up. While the tower is hot enough, it converts a fluid piped into it into a hotter fluid piped out, which then drives power generation elsewhere.

[NOTE]
====
*In-game description* +
"The Solar Power Tower acts as a boiler converting water into steam or molten salt to hot molten salt. Place multiple mirrors to increase the heat level of this tower."
====

== How it works

. Link xref:/rp/buildings/solarpanels/solar-power-mirror.adoc[Solar Power Mirrors] to the tower. Mirrors should be arranged into concentric rings around the tower and the mirrors will automatically link.
. During the day each linked mirror with a clear line of sight generates *heat*.
. Pipe the input fluid into the tower's *input pipe*. The tower automatically selects the matching recipe (Water or Molten Salt).
. As long as tower heat is at or above the recipe's minimum, the tower converts the fluid and pushes the result out of its *output pipe*.

Production runs at a *flat rate* whenever the tower is hot enough. Extra heat beyond the recipe minimum does not increase throughput, but it keeps the tower producing for a longer as the suns set.

== Heat, mirrors, and the two recipes

Each xref:/rp/buildings/solarpanels/solar-power-mirror.adoc[Solar Power Mirror] contributes up to *15 heat* at solar noon, scaled down by how high the sun is (0 at night). The tower's heat is the sum of its mirrors' heat, clamped to a maximum of *600*.

The tower has two recipes, each requiring a minimum tower heat to run:

[%header,cols="2,2,2,1,2"]
|===
| Recipe | Input | Output | Rate | Min. tower heat

| Water -> Steam
| Water
| High Pressure Steam
| 120{nbsp}m³/min
| 200 (≈ 14 mirrors at noon)

| Molten Salt -> Hot Molten Salt
| Molten Salt
| Hot Molten Salt
| 120{nbsp}m³/min
| 300 (≈ 20 mirrors at noon)
|===

[NOTE]
====
The "mirrors at noon" figures are the number of mirrors needed to reach that heat level *at midday*. Because mirror heat scales with the height of the sun, a field sized only for the minimum will produce for just a short window around noon. Building more mirrors keeps the tower above the threshold for a much larger part of the day.
====

== The two power chains

The tower does not generate power itself — it produces a fluid that is used elsewhere:

*Steam chain* (Water -> Steam mode):

[none]
* Solar Power Tower (Water -> *High Pressure Steam*, 120{nbsp}m³/min) -> xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Turbines.adoc[Modular Power Turbine] (High Pressure Steam -> Low Steam) -> xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Generators.adoc[Modular Power Generator] -> MW.

In this mode the tower is a drop-in replacement for a xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Boilers.adoc[Modular Power Boiler]: it makes the exact same High Pressure Steam a boiler does, but from sunlight instead of burning fuel for heat.

*Molten salt chain* (Molten Salt -> Hot Molten Salt mode):

[none]
* Solar Power Tower (Molten Salt -> *Hot Molten Salt*, 120{nbsp}m³/min) -> xref:/rp/buildings/modularpower/MP-Heaters.adoc[Solution Heater] (Hot Molten Salt -> Molten Salt + heat) -> boiler/turbine/generator chain -> MW.

This is a *closed loop*: the Solution Heater extracts the heat the tower stored in the salt and returns cool Molten Salt to be piped back to the tower. It lets a solar field feed the Modular Power steam system without burning any fuel. One tower (120{nbsp}m³/min Hot Molten Salt) can supply two Solution Heaters running the Molten Salt recipe (60{nbsp}m³/min each).

== Tips

* A tower does nothing without mirrors, and mirrors only work in daylight. Plan Solar Power Towers for daytime power and pair them with another generator type or storage for night.
* Aim for *40 mirrors* or more per tower. Thit maxes out the heat at solar noon and gives the lengthens daily production window. Fewer mirrors still work but only produce around midday.
* Keep the area around the tower clear so mirrors keep line of sight.
* Molten Salt mode is a closed loop; you only need to charge the loop with Molten Salt once, then recirculate it between the tower and Solution Heaters.
