= Farming Plots

Plots are where crops grow. A plot is placed on a xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlatform.adoc[Farming Platform], fed seeds, and left to grow its crop over time. There are three plot types: *Crop*, *Mycelia*, and *Tree*, and three xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[modules] that upgrade any plot (Irrigation, Greenhouse, Harvester).

[NOTE]
====
*In-game description (Crop Plot)* +
"Used to grow all types of crops."
====

== How growing works

* A plot must sit on a powered xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlatform.adoc[Farming Platform] (its "master" building). It also needs any required xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[modules] attached for the selected *growth recipe* before it will run.
* You select what to grow and feed the plot the matching *seeds*. The plot runs that crop's *growth recipe*. All recipes consume seeds and, over the crop's *growth time*, produce the harvested crop plus a *Biomass Capsule*.
* Every crop has *three* recipes: a *normal* one (seeds only), a *water-enriched* one (seeds + Water) for a modest boost, and a *fertilized* one (seeds + Fertilizer) that grows a much larger batch. Supplying Water or Fertilizer via an xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Irrigation Module] lets the plot run the enriched or fertilized recipe. See the full xref:/ff/buildings/Crops.adoc[crop reference] for every crop's numbers.
* Output can be picked up manually or sent to a belt automatically with a xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Harvester Module].

=== Worked example: Wheat

[%header,cols="2,3,2,1"]
|===
| Recipe | Input | Output | Time

| Wheat (normal)
| 4x Wheat Seed
| 1x Wheat + 1x Biomass Capsule
| 12{nbsp}s

| Wheat (water-enriched)
| 5x Wheat Seed + 10{nbsp}000x Water
| 2x Wheat + 2x Biomass Capsule
| 10{nbsp}s

| Wheat (fertilized)
| 15x Wheat Seed + 7{nbsp}500x Fertilizer
| 10x Wheat + 5x Biomass Capsule
| 30{nbsp}s
|===

That works out to *5 Wheat/min* (normal), *12 Wheat/min* (water-enriched), and *20 Wheat/min* (fertilized). The fertilized recipe is roughly *4x* the normal output for the cost of Fertilizer and more seeds. Other crops follow the same pattern with their own numbers (fluid amounts are in millilitres: 10{nbsp}000 = 10{nbsp}m³).

[NOTE]
====
*Seeds cannot be grown*, so you need an ongoing seed supply. Seeds are produced by the xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor], which turns *dirt products* into random seeds. Plan a dirt/seed pipeline for your farms.
====

| Mycelia
| Tier 4

| Tree Plot
| Tree fruit — Apple and Banana (only these are proven in the recipe data)
| Tier 6
|===

All three share the same build cost: *40x Dirt + 8x Reinforced Iron Plate*. ("Dirt" is a Ficsit Farming resource.)

== Modules

Plots are upgraded with three snap-on xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[modules]: *Irrigation* (supplies water/Fertilizer, enabling the enriched and fertilized recipes), *Harvester* (auto-outputs to a belt), and *Greenhouse* (faster growth). See the xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Farming Modules] page for their effects, costs, and unlock tiers.

== Tips

* *Fertilize for throughput.* The fertilized recipes yield several times more per cycle; once you have a xref:/ff/buildings/Composter.adoc[Composter] making Fertilizer, pipe it in through an Irrigation Module.
* *Keep seeds flowing.* Seeds come from the xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor] processing dirt products (not from the crops), so feed it a steady dirt supply to avoid starving your plots.
* *Automate early.* A Harvester Module turns a plot from a manual-pickup chore into a hands-off producer.
* *Pack plots onto platforms.* A xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlatform.adoc[Farming Platform Mk3] holds 9 plots on one footprint.
