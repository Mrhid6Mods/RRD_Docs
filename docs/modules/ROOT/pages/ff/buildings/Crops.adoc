= Crop Reference

Every crop in Ficsit Farming is grown from *seeds* in a xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[plot], and every crop has the same set of recipes with the same numbers — only the crop, its seed, and which plot type it uses change. This page is the full reference: which crop grows where, and the exact seed-in / crop-out figures for the *normal*, *fertilised*, and *enriched (water)* recipes.

[NOTE]
====
*Fluid amounts.* Fertilizer and Water are fluids; their recipe amounts are given in *litres*. A value of `7500` is *7.5{nbsp}m³* and `10000` is *10{nbsp}m³*. 
====

== Crops and where they grow

Each crop's *normal* recipe is locked to a specific plot type:

[%header,cols="2,2,2,1"]
|===
| Crop | Seed | Plot | Crop-recipe unlock

| Wheat | Wheat Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 2
| Carrot | Carrot Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 2
| Corn | Corn Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 2
| Potato | Potato Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 2
| Cotton | Cotton Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 4
| Pumpkin | Pumpkin Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 4
| Yosse Berry | Yosse Seed | Crop Plot | Tier 4
| Mycelia | Mycelia Seed | Mycelia Plot | Tier 4
| Apple | Apple Seed | Tree Plot | Tier 6
| Banana | Banana Seed | Tree Plot | Tier 6
|===

[NOTE]
====
*Tree Plot grows Apple and Banana,* these are the only two crops the Tree Plot can grow. There are no separate wood / leaves / generic "fruit" crops, so despite the "tree products" wording elsewhere, only Apple and Banana are confirmed for the Tree Plot.
====

== The three recipe types

Every crop above has *three* recipes, and the numbers are *identical for all crops,* only the crop/seed item differs. All produce a xref:/ff/buildings/Composter.adoc[Biomass Capsule] as a by-product.

[%header,cols="2,3,3,1,2"]
|===
| Recipe | Input | Output | Time | Throughput

| Normal
| 4x _Seed_
| 1x _Crop_ + 1x Biomass Capsule
| 12{nbsp}s
| 5 crop/min

| Fertilised
| 15x _Seed_ + 7{nbsp}500x Fertilizer (7.5{nbsp}m³)
| 10x _Crop_ + 5x Biomass Capsule
| 30{nbsp}s
| 20 crop/min

| Enriched (water)
| 5x _Seed_ + 10{nbsp}000x Water (10{nbsp}m³)
| 2x _Crop_ + 2x Biomass Capsule
| 10{nbsp}s
| 12 crop/min
|===

* *Crops do not provide seeds,* you must continually supply new seeds from a xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor]. 
* *Normal* crops need only seeds.
* *Fertilised* crops are fed Fertilizer through an xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Irrigation Module] and give *4x* the throughput of the normal recipe (20 vs 5 crop/min) for the cost of Fertilizer and more seeds. Fertilised recipes unlock at Tier 6.
* *Enriched (water)* is fed Water through an Irrigation Module. At 12 crop/min it sits between the normal and fertilised recipes and uses no Fertilizer. Enriched recipes unlock alongside the Irrigation Module at Tier 3 (Wheat/Carrot/Corn/Potato) with the rest following at Tiers 5–6.

[NOTE]
====
*Worked example — Wheat (matches the numbers on the xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[Plots] page):*

* Normal: 4 Wheat Seed -> 1 Wheat + 1 Biomass Capsule in 12{nbsp}s = *5 Wheat/min*.
* Fertilised: 15 Wheat Seed + 7.5{nbsp}m³ Fertilizer -> 10 Wheat + 5 Biomass Capsule in 30{nbsp}s = *20 Wheat/min*.

Substitute any crop's own name and seed for identical numbers.
====

[NOTE]
====
*Remember your seeds.* Seeds come from the xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor], which turns *dirt* into seeds, Plan a dirt and seed supply before placing a field.
====

== Mycelia and Tree plots

* *Mycelia Plot* grows *Mycelia*. Its normal recipe is 4 Mycelia Seed -> 1 Mycelia + 1 Biomass Capsule in 12{nbsp}s, and its fertilised recipe is 15 Mycelia Seed + 7.5{nbsp}m³ Fertilizer -> 10 Mycelia + 5 Biomass Capsule in 30{nbsp}s, the same numbers as every other crop. Mycelia feeds xref:/ff/buildings/DoggoHouse.adoc[Doggo Chow] and pies/muffins in the xref:/ff/buildings/CookingHub.adoc[Cooking Hub].
* *Tree Plot* grows *Apple* and *Banana* only, using the standard normal/fertilised/enriched numbers above.
