= Cooking Hub

The Cooking Hub is where crops become *food*. It takes harvested crops (plus Water) and cooks them into bread, muffins, pies, and the xref:/ff/buildings/DoggoHouse.adoc[Doggo House]'s Doggo Chow. It is the "one stop shop" cooking building, unlocked at Tier 4.

[NOTE]
====
*In-game description* +
"Used to cook, Bake, Shake, Boil, Fry, Blend every food and drink to your hearts desire. This is the one stop shop for cooking any food you want"
====

[NOTE]
====
*Water is a fluid* whose recipe amounts are given in litres. `20000` is *20{nbsp}m³* and `25000` is *25{nbsp}m³*.
====

== How it works

* Feed the Cooking Hub the crop ingredients for a recipe plus *Water*; it cooks the recipe over its cycle time and outputs the finished food.
* All food recipes below are produced in the Cooking Hub, including Doggo Chow.
* Wheat is the backbone ingredient — bread, every muffin, and every pie all use Wheat, so a steady Wheat supply feeds the whole menu.

== Milestones

[%header,cols="1,2"]
|===
| | Cooking Hub

| Available with
| HUB Tier 4 — "Sous Chef" milestone
|===

The Cooking Hub unlocks at *Tier 4* on the "Sous Chef" milestone, together with Bread, Wheat Muffin, and Corn Muffin. The remaining recipes come later: the *pies* and *Banana Bread* arrive at Tier 6 ("Head Chef"), and *Doggo Chow* unlocks at Tier 6 alongside the xref:/ff/buildings/DoggoHouse.adoc[Doggo House] ("Rancher").

== Tips

* *Wheat first.* Every bread, muffin, and pie needs Wheat — the muffins and pies each cnsume 60–100 Wheat per cycle, so scale Wheat plots before scaling the kitchen.
* *Pies are ingredient-hungry.* A pie needs 60 Wheat + 80 fruit per 120{nbsp}s cycle; plan for large fertilised fields if you want steady pie output.
* *Feed the doggos here.* Route some Yosse Berry and Mycelia into a Doggo Chow line if you are running a xref:/ff/buildings/DoggoHouse.adoc[Doggo House].
