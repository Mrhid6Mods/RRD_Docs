= Ficsit Farming

== Introduction:
Ficsit Pioneers can get their hands dirty with the Ficsit Farming Mod, automating the production of crops and cooked foods and looking after their friendly doggos in the Doggo house!

== Multiplayer support:

Ficsit Farming will have 100% multiplayer support. We aim to fully support multiplayer.
If you encounter any bug related to multiplayer please report these on our Discord Server.

== What this Mod does:

This mod adds the following:

* New Buildings
* New Items/Fluids
* Milestones
* New game mechanics for Doggos

What this mod will not add:

* MAM research
* Vehicles
* A Cow

== The farm loop:

At its core, Ficsit Farming is a cycle:

[none]
* *Seeds* -> xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[Plot] (grows over time) -> *Crop* (+ Biomass Capsule)
* *Dirt* -> xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor] -> *random Seeds* (seeds come from processing dirt, not from the crops themselves)
* Crops feed the xref:/ff/buildings/CookingHub.adoc[Cooking Hub] (-> *food*) and the xref:/ff/buildings/Composter.adoc[Composter] (-> *fertilizer*).
* *Fertilizer* fed back into a plot unlocks the much higher-yield *fertilized* recipes.

Plots are mounted on a xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlatform.adoc[Farming Platform] and can be upgraded with three *modules*: Irrigation (supplies water/fertilizer), Greenhouse (faster growth), and Harvester (automatic output).

== Buildings at a glance:

[%header,cols="2,3,1"]
|===
| Building | Role | Unlocks at

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlatform.adoc[Farming Platform Mk1/2/3]
| Foundation that houses plots (1 / 4 / 9 plots)
| Tier 2 / 3 / 7

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[Crop Plot]
| Grows all standard crops from seeds
| Tier 2

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[Mycelia Plot]
| Grows Mycelia
| Tier 4

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingPlots.adoc[Tree Plot]
| Grows tree fruit (Apple, Banana)
| Tier 6

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Irrigation Module]
| Supplies water / fertilizer to a plot
| Tier 3

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Greenhouse Module]
| Speeds up crop growth
| Tier 4

| xref:/ff/buildings/FarmingModules.adoc[Harvester Module]
| Automates harvesting (outputs to belt)
| Tier 3

| xref:/ff/buildings/SeedExtractor.adoc[Seed Extractor]
| Turns dirt products into random seeds
| Tier 2

| xref:/ff/buildings/Composter.adoc[Composter]
| Turns crops/biomass into fertilizer
| Tier 5

| xref:/ff/buildings/CookingHub.adoc[Cooking Hub]
| Cooks crops into food (bread, pies, muffins…)
| Tier 4

| xref:/ff/buildings/DoggoHouse.adoc[Doggo House]
| Houses a Lizard Doggo; consumes Doggo Chow
| Tier 6
|===

The whole system is gated behind the mod's own HUB milestones (labelled by tier), starting at *Tier 2* with the Crop Plot, Farming Platform Mk1, and Seed Extractor, enough to start a basic farm.
